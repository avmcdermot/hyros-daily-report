Notable Patterns & Actionable Insights
1 Multi-year upgrades are the revenue engine. The 3-Year and 2-Year upgrades combined for $790 (75.4% of net revenue) from just 4 customers. Focus upsell flow optimizations — and any A/B tests via Optimizely — on surfacing multi-year offers immediately post-checkout.
2 Syndication partners (snapi & TechInvestorNews) are punching above their weight. These two sources alone drove $474 (45.2% of total revenue) via the partner_feed campaign. Recommend increasing content syndication volume through these partners — they are clearly delivering high-intent buyers.
3 Mobile dominates (83%) — ensure checkout UX is mobile-first. 5 of 6 purchases were on mobile. With all traffic entering through a single page (/benzinga-edge-4), any friction on mobile — slow load, form issues, paywall rendering — directly caps revenue. Audit the mobile checkout flow on this page as a priority.
