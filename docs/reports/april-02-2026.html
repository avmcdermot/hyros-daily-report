Notable Patterns & Actionable Insights
1 Partner Feed Dominates Revenue
The partner_feed UTM campaign drove 4 purchases ($512 — 61.9% of total revenue) via sofi.com and robinhood.com equally ($256 each). These partner integrations are the highest-leverage acquisition channel today. Prioritize maintaining and expanding these partner placements.
2 3-Year Upgrades Punching Above Their Weight
Only 2 of 9 customers (22%) bought the 3-Year Upgrade, yet that product contributed $474 — 57.3% of net revenue. The AOV of the upgrade cohort is $237 vs. $91.89 overall. Upsell prompts and upgrade CTAs are delivering outsized value; consider A/B testing more aggressive upgrade placement.
3 ChatGPT as an Emerging Referral Source
One purchase arrived via chatgpt.com — an AI-driven referral that signals organic brand visibility in LLM outputs. While small today, this channel warrants monitoring as a zero-cost inbound signal. Ensure Benzinga Edge content is well-optimized for AI answer engines.
